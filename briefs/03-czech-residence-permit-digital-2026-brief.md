### FAQ Section (5 items)
1. Can I stay in Czech Republic while my permit application is being processed? — Yes, under the "bridge provision" you can remain legally while the decision is pending.
2. How long does a Czech residence permit take to process in 2026? — Target is 30–60 days under the new digital system.
3. What is the Foreigner Account (Cizinecký účet)? — The secure online portal where all immigration applications and documents are managed.
4. Can I work in Czech Republic on a tourist visa while waiting for a permit? — No; working without the appropriate permit is a criminal offence.
5. What happens if my permit expires while I'm waiting for renewal? — Under Czech law, a timely renewal application extends your legal stay automatically.
