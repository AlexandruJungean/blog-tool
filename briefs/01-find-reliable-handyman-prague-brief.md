### FAQ Section (4 items)
1. Do I need to speak Czech to hire a handyman in Prague? — No; look specifically for platforms filtering by English-speaking providers.
2. Is it normal to pay cash upfront in Prague? — A small deposit (20–30%) is normal; full upfront payment is a red flag.
3. How long does it take to get a handyman in Prague? — On vetted platforms, average response is 24–48 hours; urgent jobs within the day are possible but cost more.
4. Can a landlord's handyman work on my flat without my consent? — Czech tenancy law gives tenants the right to consent; cite Občanský zákoník §2207.
